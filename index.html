The Eternal Story

The Eternal Story has no start and no ending, it was here all along, and Gareth deep inside knew it but couldn't remember given all the shots he'd downed last night. There was a number though, sharpied in his hand. Yeah, he'd lost his cellphone and wrote Grace's number on his hand. He dragged his body across the bed and reached for the bottle of water from last week.
